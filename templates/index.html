Welcome to the Green Builders Comunity.

This is your site to learn, share and chat about small sustainable houses!

The common objective of green buildings is to reduce the overall impact of the built environment on human health and the natural environment by:

Sustainable energy and natural resources use

Efficiently using energy, water, and other resources

Reducing environmental impact.

Protecting your family and enviroment health.

Sustainable building materials

Reducing waste, pollution and environmental degradation. Re-using materials.

Sustainable energy use

Heating, ventilation and cooling system efficiency

Sustainable building materials

Recycled materials. Lower volatile organic compounds. Materials sustainability standards

Waste management, reducing environmental impact

Every building should be as small as possible and not to contribute to sprawl, even if the most energy-efficient, environmentally sound methods are used in design and construction.
